Our Story

Construction done the way it should be

Naouali & Co was founded on a simple belief: a remodel or a build should be something you look forward to, not something you dread. Too many people have a story about a contractor who rushed the work, blew the budget, or stopped answering the phone — and we wanted to be the opposite of that.

So we built a company around craftsmanship, clear communication and genuine care. We take the time to understand each client, treat every home and business like our own, and see every project through from the first conversation to the final detail. It's why so much of our work comes from referrals and repeat clients.
